About us

CBS Re-Creations LLC is a small, family-owned business that owns and manages residential rental properties in the Oklahoma City, Oklahoma area. We take pride in providing well-maintained homes and being responsive, fair landlords to our tenants.

Our properties

We own and manage a small portfolio of single-family rental homes in south Oklahoma City neighborhoods. We keep our portfolio small on purpose: it lets the family personally handle maintenance, tenant relationships, and day-to-day management rather than outsourcing to a property-management company.

Rent payments

Our tenants pay rent securely online through Zillow Rental Manager. The business uses automated text notifications (see our SMS policy) to keep our own management team informed about payment status.

Interested in renting?

When a home becomes available we list it on Zillow. If you'd like to be notified about future availability, email us and we'll keep you in mind.

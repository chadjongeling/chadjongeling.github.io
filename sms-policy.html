Program description

CBS Re-Creations LLC sends automated SMS notifications about rent payment status and property operations from our business toll-free number. Messages are sent only to members of our own management team who have opted in via our opt-in form. We do not send marketing messages, and we do not message tenants, customers, or the general public through this program.

Message frequency

Approximately 10–30 messages per month: confirmations that rent was paid, notices that a deposit arrived, monthly lease status summaries, and alerts when a rent payment is late or doesn't match the expected amount.

Fees, opt-out, and help

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier's plan. Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages. Reply HELP for help, or contact cbsken@yahoo.com. Consent is not a condition of any purchase, employment, or tenancy.

Privacy

Phone numbers collected for this program are used solely to deliver the notifications described above. We do not sell, rent, or share mobile numbers or opt-in information with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Who receives these messages

CBS Re-Creations LLC sends automated SMS notifications about rent payment status for our rental properties. These messages go only to the four owners and managers of the business itself — they are internal operational notifications. No tenant, customer, or member of the public receives these messages.

How consent is collected

Each team member opts in through our SMS opt-in form, which records their name, mobile number, and express consent to the program terms. The consent record is delivered by email to the program administrator and kept on file by the business. Any team member can stop the messages at any time by replying STOP or by asking the administrator, and can rejoin via the opt-in form.

What is sent

Approximately 10–30 messages per month: confirmations that rent was paid, notices that a deposit arrived at the bank, and alerts when a rent payment is late. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.
